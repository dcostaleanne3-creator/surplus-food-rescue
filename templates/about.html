Our Mission

Bridging the gap between excess food and empty plates in Hubballi through technology and community action.

The Problem We're Solving

Every day in Hubballi, hundreds of kilograms of perfectly good, nutritious food from weddings, corporate events, and restaurants end up in landfills, while simultaneously, vulnerable communities skip meals.

The primary issue isn't a lack of food—it's a massive logistical failure. We saw the need for a real-time, highly efficient network to bridge this gap.

"If we can rescue just 25% of the food wasted at large events, we can eliminate hunger in our immediate districts."

Our Core Values

Speed & Efficiency

Using AI and instant notifications, we ensure food is rescued and redistributed before it loses nutritional value or spoils.

Community First

We partner exclusively with verified local NGOs who have deep roots in the communities they serve.

Sustainability

Beyond feeding people, our mission is to dramatically reduce the methane emissions caused by food rot in municipal landfills.
