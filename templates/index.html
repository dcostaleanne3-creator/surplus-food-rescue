How it works

A seamless process to stop waste

We use predictive AI to route food efficiently and safely before it spoils.

1. Log Surplus

Got extra food from an event or restaurant? Simply fill out our quick form with the quantity and type of food.

2. AI Analysis

Our system instantly analyzes the data, predicts waste urgency, and automatically identifies the nearest matched NGOs.

3. Swift Rescue

Vetted partners are alerted instantly to pick up the high-volume surplus and deliver it to those in need.

Resource Center

Food Rescue Guidelines

Master the best practices of keeping surplus food safe, viable, and ready for redistribution.

Cold Chain

Maintain perishables below 5°C. Continuous temperature control prevents bacterial growth.

Hygiene Prep

Use FDA-approved containers. Never cross-contaminate raw and cooked surplus before donation.

Rapid Transit

AI assigns nearest verified NGO ensuring delivery within 2 hours of packaging to avoid spoilage.

Last Mile

Remaining inedible inventory is routed directly to municipal composting streams.
